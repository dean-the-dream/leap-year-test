Did you know!?

It takes 365 days for the Earth to make one complete revolution around the sun. Well, it's actually more lke 65 days, 5 hours, 59 minutes and 16 seconds. Every 4 years, the extra time adds up to a whole day.

To ensure that the times and seasons stay the same from year to year, we add an additional day to the end of February. Every 4 years, February has 29 days instead of 28. We call this a Leap Year!
